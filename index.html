Welcome to SVC College of Engineering and Technology

SVC College of Engineering and Technology (SVCET), established in 2001, is dedicated to providing quality technical education and holistic growth in the heart of Tamil Nadu. Affiliated to Anna University, we emphasize academic excellence, industry partnerships, and a nurturing campus environment.
